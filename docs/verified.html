About Verification

Welcome to the documentation for the Verification System used in the DarkPurpleOF's Website.

Introduction to getting verified

The verification system enables users to prove their identity and prevent fakers within the platform.

Proof of Valid Profile

The verification system ensures that verified accounts are the real accounts of those users.

Benefits

Verified users may receive early access, enhanced support, pre-alpha content, and partnership opportunities.

Important Notice: Faking a verified user

Faking or impersonating a verified user is a major offense. Reports or automated detection of impersonation or solicitation will result in immediate and permanent ban from the platform.
